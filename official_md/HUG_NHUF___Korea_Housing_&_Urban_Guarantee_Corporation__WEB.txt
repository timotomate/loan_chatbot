Expected Effects
1. Diversified methods to supply rental housing such as rental housing REITs through flexible operation of NHUF
2. Improvement of housing environment for small-size housing residents through rehabilitation of run-down private houses and financial assistance for small-sized rental housing construction
3. Landscape improvement of commercial facilities and vitalization of community groups formed by residents
4. Vitalized economic activities through building an economic foothold including urban complex, etc.

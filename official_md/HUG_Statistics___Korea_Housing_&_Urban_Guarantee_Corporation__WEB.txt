HUG YOU
We will support your dream of homeownership.
Since its foundation as the one and only housing guarantor in 1993, Korea Housing and Urban Guarantee Corporation has provided guarantees to 9.18 million households amounting to 740 trillion KRW, thereby contributing to the growth of Korean housing industry, the improvement of housing welfare of the public, and the stabilization of housing finance market.
We are here as your friend helping you achieve your dream of homeownership, and as a trustworthy partner for your housing business.
Guarantees for Corporate Customer(20 products)
Guarantee for Housing Completion, Guarantee for Housing Project Finance (standard PF loan), Guarantee for Build-Then-Sell Housing Construction Loan, Guarantee for Defect Repairs, etc.
Guarantees for Individual Customer(8 products)
Guarantee for a Refund of Jeonse Deposit Loan (Guarantee for a Refund of Jeonse Deposit), Guarantee for Housing Purchase Loan, Guarantee for Housing Redevelopment Loan, etc.
Other Businesses
Entrustment of financing (redevelopment project, remodeling loan, housing rehabilitation loan), entrustment of profit (loss) sharing mortgage program, etc.

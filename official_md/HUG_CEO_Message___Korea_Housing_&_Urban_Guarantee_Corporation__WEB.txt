We are committed to improving the quality of life for the people by providing financial support to enhance housing welfare and promote urban regeneration.
We warmly welcome you all to the official website of the Korea Housing & Urban Guarantee Corporation (HUG).
Since our establishment in 1993 as a specialized housing guarantee organization, HUG took a significant leap forward in 2015 as the public body exclusively managing the Housing and Urban Fund under the Housing and Urban Fund Act.
Throughout our history, HUG has reliably supported the dream of homeownership and ensured a steady supply of housing through various guarantee products for businesses and individuals, including Guarantee for Housing Completion and Guarantee for Jeonse Deposit Return. As the sole manager of the Housing and Urban Fund, we have dedicated ourselves to stabilizing the housing market and fostering national housing stability.
Moving forward, HUG will focus on four strategic pillars: strengthening the housing safety net for ordinary families, building a foundation for robust housing supply, leading ESG management, and establishing a sustainable innovation framework. We will remain a trusted partner, standing by the people to further enhance and expand access to affordable and reliable housing for all.
Thank you.
In Ho Choi President & CEO Korea Housing & Urban Guarantee Corporation

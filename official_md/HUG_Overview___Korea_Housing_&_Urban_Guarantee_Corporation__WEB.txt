Korea Housing & Urban Guarantee Corporation
HUG offers various types of housing guarantees and implements government policies to improve people's residential welfare and encourage urban regeneration. It will help achieve housing stability for all through effective operation and management of NHUF.
HUG will strive to become a trustworthy corporation by making consistent efforts for management innovation and productive revenue management.
Basis for Foundation
HUG was established in accordance with Article 16 of NHUF Act
Purpose of Foundation
HUG was founded to improve housing well-being and revitalize urban renewal projects, thereby contributing to a better quality of life of the public by providing various guarantees, implementing national projects, and effectively operating and managing NHUF
Main Business Areas
Guarantee business for housing that includes guarantee for housing completion, guarantee for rental deposit, guarantee for cooperative housing completion, etc.
Guarantee business in support of national policies, including the guarantee for a refund of Jeonse deposit, guarantee for mortgage, etc.
Business entrusted by national or local governments, including the entrustment of profit (loss) sharing mortgage program, etc.
Business involving the operation and management of NHUF under NHUF Act

HUG the People We hold homes, cities, and people and take off with a new vision.
Building a world of abundance by adding value to people’s lives with better housing and cities is the future that HUG envisions. We will strive to become a leader in housing and urban finance with the confidence and love of people. We will embrace the needs of all Koreans by constantly changing and growing to bring new hope to your space of life.
MISSON Contribute to raising quality of people’s lives with providing financial support for housing welfare improvement and urban regeneration revitalization

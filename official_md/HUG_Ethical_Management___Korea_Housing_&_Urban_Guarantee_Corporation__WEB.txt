HUG will be a leader in ethical management.
HUG has put its priority on ethical and win-win management and set up the objectives of establishing an advanced ethical management system, internalizing ethical corporate culture, raising its stature as an ethical company, and disseminating ethical corporate culture companywide.
To achieve the objectives, HUG has established the norms for ethical management, provided practical trainings, fostered ethical corporate culture that values mutual cooperation, and established an effective monitoring system. Moreover, it has created a department dedicated to ethical management for systematic ethical operation, which supports ethical activities of HUG.

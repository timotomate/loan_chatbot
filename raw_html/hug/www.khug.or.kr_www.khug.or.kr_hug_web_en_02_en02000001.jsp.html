From the commencement of construction to move-in,
HUG will be by
your side as an assuring partner.

1. Approval for a Project Plan (Construction permit, Project go-ahead)

PF Guarantee
It guarantees repayment of the principal and accrued interest of housing PF loan for the project cost including the cost of land that developer receives by offering future cash flow and business feasibility as a collateral in return.
Guarantee for Housing Construction Loan Covered by National Housing & Urban Fund
It guarantees the repayment of a quasi-housing construction loan provided by National Housing & Urban Fund entrusted institution in cases where the developer fails to repay the loan
Guarantee for License and Permission
It guarantees the fulfillment of requirements imposed by the state or local government for housing project license and permission in cases where a project owner fails to fulfil the requirement
Guarantee for Deposit of Supervising Expense
It guarantees the payment to a supervisor in cases where a project owner fails to make the payment to the supervisor regarding the supervision of applicable project.
Guarantee for Small-scale Housing Redevelopment Loan
It guarantees the payment of the principal and interest of redevelopment loan (that covers the relocation cost, shared expense, operating cost) borrowed by a housing cooperative (members of a cooperative) for redevelopment and maintenance in cases where the cooperative fails to repay the loan.
Guarantee for Remodeling Loan
It guarantees the payment of the principal and interest of remodeling loan (that covers the relocation cost, shared expense, operating cost) borrowed by a housing cooperative (members of a cooperative) for remodeling project in cases where the cooperative fails to repay the loan.

2. Commencement of Construction

Guarantee for Housing Completion
It guarantees the completion of housing construction or the refund of down payment and middle payments made in cases where a project owner fails to fulfil its obligations under pre-sale agreement due to bankruptcy, insolvency, etc.
Guarantee for Completion of Multi-purpose housing
It guarantees the completion of construction of multi-purpose housing or the refund of down payment and middle payments made in cases where a developer or project owner fails to fulfil its obligations under pre-sale agreement due to bankruptcy, insolvency, etc.
Guarantee for Rental Housing Completion
It guarantees the completion of rental housing construction or the refund of down payment and middle payments made in cases where a project owner fails to fulfil its obligations under rental agreement due to its bankruptcy, insolvency, etc.
Guarantee for Studio Apartment Completion
It guarantee the completion of construction (including pre-use inspection and ownership preservation registration) of studio apartment (including other facilities that are built in the same building as studio apartment) or the refund of down payment and middle payments made in cases where a project owner fails to fulfil its obligations under pre-sale agreement due to bankruptcy, insolvency, etc.
Guarantee for Cooperative Housing Completion
It guarantees the completion of cooperative housing construction or the payment of damages incurred by the cooperative in cases where a constructor of cooperative housing project fails to fulfil its obligation of construction.
Guarantee for Subcontract Payment
It guarantees the payment to a subcontractor in charge of subcontracted work (entrustment of construction) prescribed by the Fair Transactions in Subcontracting Act in cases where the constructor of a housing project fails to pay the subcontractor.

3. Approval for a Public Notice for Recruitment

Guarantee for Housing Purchase Loan
It guarantees the repayment of housing purchase loan from a financial institution that the home buyer received for the house guaranteed for its completion.
Guarantee for Rental Housing Lease Loan
It guarantees the repayment of rental deposit loan from a financial institution that the tenant received for the rental housing.
Guarantee for Rental Deposit
It guarantees the lease of rental housing by a private constructor or liability of the rental deposit (including the pre-use inspection and the registration of ownership preservation) pursuant to Article 49 Clause 1 and the Enforcement Decree Article 38 o「f Special Act on Private Rental Housing」

4. Under Construction

Guarantee for Build-Then-Sell Housing Construction Loan

  • It guarantees the payment of the principal and interest of housing construction loan under the condition that developer allots part of housing units for after-sale in cases where the developer fails to repay the loan.

5. Pre-use Inspection (approval for use)

Guarantee for Defect Repairs

  • It guarantees the payment of defect repair cost in cases where a project owner or constructor (building owner) fails to repair defects.

6. Registration of Ownership Preservation

7. Management after Move-in

Guarantee for a Refund of Jeonse Deposit
It guarantees the return of Jeonse deposit to lessee upon termination of Jeonse lease agreement, if the lessor fails to return the deposit to lessee.
Guarantee for Jeonse Deposit Loan
It guarantees both the payment of the principal and interest of Jeonse deposit loan to financial institution (Guarantee for the repayment of Jeonse Deposit Loan under special conditions), and the return of Jeonse deposit to lessee (Guarantee for a Refund of Jeonse Deposit) in cases where the principal debtor fails to fulfil his/her obligation.
Guarantee for Mortgage
It guarantees the payment of the principal and interest of a loan after having unsold, completed units owned by the housing development as collateral.
Guarantee for a Refund of Jeonse Deposit for Jeonse Rental Housing
It guarantees the return of Jeonse deposit to Jeonse sublease business operator with existing housing units financed by the National Housing & Urban Fund in cases where the lessor fails to return the Jeonse deposit.
Guarantee for Payment of Rent
It guarantees the payment of monthly rent to a lessor in cases where a lessee fails to pay the monthly rent under lease agreement.
Guarantee for Management of Rental Housing
It guarantees both the payment of rent to a lessor (Guarantee for Payment of Rent for Sublease Business Operator) and the return of rental deposit to a lessee (Guarantee for Return of Rental Deposit for Sublease Business Operator) in cases where the self-administered Sublease business operator fails to fulfil his/her obligations.
Guarantee for Home Purchase for Rental Purposes
It guarantees the payment of the principal and interest of a home purchase loan that rental business operator (including the corporate-type rental business operator) took out for the purpose of rental business in cases where the operator fails to repay the loan.
Guarantee for Jeonse Deposit Loan Covered by National Housing & Urban Fund
It guarantees the payment of the principal and interest of an studio apartment Jeonse deposit loan covered National Housing & Urban Fund in cases where the lessee fails to repay the loan.

Financial guarantee for urban regeneration

Urban Regeneration PF Guarantee
It guarantees repayment of the urban regeneration PF loan for the project cost including the cost of land that developer receives by offering future cash flow and business feasibility as a collateral in return.
Guarantee for Urban Regeneration Loan
It guarantees repayment of the urban regeneration remodeling loan for the project cost that developer receives by offering their own building as a collateral in return.
Guarantee for Urban Regeneration Facility and Operation Loan
It guarantees repayment of the loan for the facility and operation of the village and social enterprises (cooperative) with the building or credit for the refund of Jeonse deposit as a collateral in return.
Guarantee for Consumer-oriented Urban Regeneration Support Fund
It guarantees repayment of the principal of the loan in order for the consumer-oriented urban regeneration businesses to be financed from the NHUF.
Guarantee for Urban Block Renewal Project Loan (initial project cost)
It guarantees repayment of the principal and interest of the loan for the project executors to be financed via NHUF or financial institutions.
Guarantee for Autonomous Housing Improvement Project Loan
It guarantees repayment of the principal and interest of the loan for the project executors to be financed via NHUF or financial institutions.
